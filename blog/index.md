# The Trials of Hercules

Welcome to a long-form retelling of the Twelve Labors of Hercules — penance imposed on the hero by King Eurystheus at the bidding of the oracle at Delphi, after Hera's madness drove him to a crime he could never undo.

Each post takes one labor and reads it as a short story. The labors are presented in their canonical order. The arc moves from beasts on Greek soil, to monsters across the Mediterranean, to the very gates of the underworld.

Start with [[the-nemean-lion]] or wander as you like.
